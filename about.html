About Us

Fostering and empowering the artificial intelligence community at Waterloo and beyond.

WAT.ai is a studnet-run design team based at the University of Waterloo. We are a passionate group of undergraduate and graduate students looking to make an impact within the field of AI. Through participation in concentrated design pods, educational resources, and industry conulting opportunities, our goals is to make AI more accessible within the Waterloo community.
